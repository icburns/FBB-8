FBB-8

Welcome!

FBB-8 replaces Facebook reactions with BB-8 icons. See changelog below for details and new features

This extension is maintained at the leisure of the developer, and may not always keep up with changes to Facebook. No user data is collected or stored by FBB-8. If you have any neat ideas, we're always open to feedback!
